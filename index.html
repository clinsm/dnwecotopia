Current 
Clients & Projects

DNW Ecotopia assists PPC cement in Pretoria, Germiston and Riebeek to maintain, modify and install dust collection systems to ensure that no unintending cement dust is emitted during the cement processing plant to pollute the environment. We perform regular plant assessments to provide the dust suppression recommendations ensure plant is running optimally.  This includes any duct modifications and installations.

PPC Cement

Project to improve their blowing system (Ducting, dust collector fan, filter bags) used to cool fan room in the metal melting point inside the plant. This was to help increase maximum operation time of the production plant 

SCAW Metal Group

We are involved in a project to renovate the main Baghouses of the Boksburg plant. Resize ducting to ensure correct suction of the material. We also help with air separation from melting and crushing to ensure its filtered using correct medium of extraction for maximum performance. We also looking into employing air canons. 

Fry's Metal

We help our client AECI to purify air, liquids, gases and steam to ensure consistent product quality and keep the equipment running smoothly while also preventing unwanted emission of the product to the environment.
