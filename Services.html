Consulting and Assessment Services

Our team of engineers and consultants offers expert guidance on air pollution control solutions, regulatory compliance, system optimization, and process improvements.

Ductwork Design and Installation

Our experts design and install ductwork systems to efficiently transport dust-laden air from various industrial processes to the baghouse or dust collector. We carefully assess the ducting requirements and customize it with accordingly with high level of accuracy. 

We prioritize proper sizing, layout optimization, and material selection to minimize pressure drops, ensure airflow balance, and maximize system performance
